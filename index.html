MENTAL HEALTH MATTERS

Never give up, your story is still being told.


By sharing our personal experiences, we can work to reduce the stigma associated with mental health within female leadership roles. People with mental health illnesses are not the only ones who can have a dialogue about mental health. Our social, emotional, physical, and cognitive well-being are all impacted. Prioritize your mental wellness because it is everything, make the most of the moment. Make your mental health a top priority since it is everything. If we start being honest about our suffering, our rage, and our flaws instead of pretending they don't exist, then together we will be able to break down the stigma against mental health and the world will be a better place than we found it.
